# FinBot Use Case Definition

## Domain: FinTech Customer Support

Fine-tuned LLM to answer questions on global financial markets, stock exchanges, and trading operations.

## 7 Key Support Scenarios

1. **Trading Issues** - Order rejections, SSR, execution errors
2. **Indices & Exchanges** - FTSE 100, S&P 500, NSE, rebalancing rules
3. **Product Comparison** - ETF vs. Mutual Funds, comparison guides
4. **Market Mechanics** - Settlement cycles, lot sizes, trading hours (HKEX, NASDAQ, etc.)
5. **KYC & Compliance** - Account setup, rejected KYC, regulatory requirements
6. **Market Events** - Interest rates, bond market, Fed decisions impact
7. **Risk Management** - Currency hedging, portfolio optimization, leverage

## Key Competencies

- 200+ exchanges & indices knowledge
- Regulatory frameworks (SEC, SEBI, FCA, SFC)
- Trading mechanics & settlement cycles
- Professional, clear communication
- Multi-domain (equities, derivatives, forex)

## Success Metrics

- Reduce support tickets by 30-40%
- 75%+ first-contact resolution
- 4.5+/5.0 CSAT score
- <5 seconds response time
